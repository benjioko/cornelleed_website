About CEED

CEED is a community of makers building creative engineering projects—blending design, fabrication, electronics, and software to bring ideas to life.

Subteams

Mechanical & Fabrication

CAD, mechanisms, prototyping, fabrication, and iteration—bringing the physical build to life.

  • CAD + assemblies
  • Mechanisms + packaging
  • Prototyping + fabrication
Electrical & Software

Power, sensing, embedded systems, and control—making the build interactive and reliable.

  • Wiring + power systems
  • Embedded + sensors
  • Control logic + testing
